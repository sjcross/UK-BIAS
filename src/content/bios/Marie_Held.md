Marie is the Bioimage Analyst at the Centre for Cell Imaging, a Shared Research Facility at the University of Liverpool. With a background in applied physics, she has developed extensive expertise in light microscopy and has worked in bioimage analysis since 2019. She supports researchers in designing robust image analysis workflows, enabling quantitative analysis of microscopy data, and promoting the adoption of FAIR principles for imaging data, analysis workflows, and scientific software. Outside of work, Marie enjoys photography and digital illustration, when home life allows.
