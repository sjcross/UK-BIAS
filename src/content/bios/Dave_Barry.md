Dave leads the Crick's BioImage Analysis Team and is also Deputy Head of the Advanced Light Microscopy STP, bringing expertise from electronic engineering and a PhD in quantitative imaging, along with six years of postdoctoral research in cell motility. He directs a team developing scalable analytical pipelines powered by machine learning and AI for biomedical imaging across 100+ research groups at the Crick. He advocates for FAIR data, open-source methodologies, and community-focused training to remove barriers to rigorous and reproducible quantitative analysis.
