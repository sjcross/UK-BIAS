Graeme is the Image Analyst at the Dundee Imaging Facility based in the Faculty of Life Sciences, University of Dundee. He has 15 years experience in this role, having started as the Image Analysis Specialist in the Micron Oxford imaging facility in 2011 before moving to the Dundee in 2014. Graeme has a background in Natural Sciences, a PhD in structural biology and a longstanding interest in computers and technology. He worked as a postdoc in the lab of Ilan Davis where he became interested in microscopy and the analysis of microscopy data. Graeme has worked on a variety of problems over the years and is well versed in the tools and techniques of bioimage data analysis.
