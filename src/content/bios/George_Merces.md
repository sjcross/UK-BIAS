George leads the Newcastle University Image Analysis Unit, coming from a background of open-source hardware development and translational medicine. His experience ranges from single-molecule tracking, live cell image analysis, tissue organisation, spatial analysis, and preclinical imaging modalities. Focusing on open-access solutions to complex problems, George and his team build protocols, pipelines, scripts, and GUIs to facilitate image analysis.
