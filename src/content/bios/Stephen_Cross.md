Stephen works at the University of Bristol's Wolfson BioImaging Facility, where he is the resident bioimage analyst. He can typically be found developing custom analysis workflows, offering advice on software or creating new analysis tools, such as the ModularImageAnalysis (MIA) plugin for Fiji.  Stephen tends to work with light microscopy images, but also dabbles with EM and macroscopic samples.
