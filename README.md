# UK-BIAS: The UK Network of BioImage Analysts

## Virtual bioimage analysis service \- open to all

Expert image analysis has become the critical bottleneck in biological research \- one that only deepens as imaging technologies continue to advance. UK-BIAS brings together leading bioimage analysis specialists from ten UK institutions, joining the UK Node of Euro-BioImaging as its first dedicated image analysis service, complementing instrument access with image data analysis capabilities.

From initial consultation to bespoke pipeline development, the network provides capacity equivalent to two dedicated full time analysts. Our team has an excellent track record in research collaboration, with outputs published in top tier journals, as well as extensive experience in designing and delivering training programmes. We are strong advocates of the FAIR principles and, as such, all our outputs are released as open-source, while researchers retain full ownership of their raw data.
